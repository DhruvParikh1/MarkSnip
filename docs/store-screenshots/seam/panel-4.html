The quiet case for plain text

Notes that outlive the app · 6 min read

For years I trusted my notes to whatever app was fashionable. Then the app shut down, and the “export” was a tangle of HTML.

Plain text is the only format that has never let me down. It opens everywhere, it diffs cleanly, and it will still open in thirty years.

The trick was never a better app. It is keeping what you write in a form you can read without anyone’s permission.
